About Us

BS Farm is an agricultural enterprise located in Jenoi Village, Rural Gambia, owned and managed by Mr. Buba Saidy. The business specializes in broiler production, egg production, and vegetable farming (okra and cucumber), with a vision of becoming a leading sustainable agribusiness in The Gambia.

Our Services

  • Broiler Chicken Production
  • Layer Egg Production
  • Vegetable Production (Okra & Cucumber)
  • Poultry Supply to Shops, Restaurants & Schools

Business Description

Business Name: BS Farm
Owner: Mr. Buba Saidy
Location: Jenoi Village, Rural Gambia
Sector: Agriculture (Poultry & Horticulture)

Mission

To produce and supply fresh, healthy and affordable poultry products and vegetables while improving food security and rural livelihoods.

Vision

To become a model agricultural enterprise in The Gambia recognized for sustainable, efficient and profitable production.

Objectives

  1. Scale poultry production.
  2. Develop irrigated vegetable farming.
  3. Strengthen marketing channels.
  4. Build a diversified agribusiness.

Market Analysis

Demand for poultry, eggs, okra and cucumber is consistently high. BS Farm benefits from market proximity, diversified income and commitment to quality.

Products & Services

  • Broilers
  • Layers
  • Fresh Eggs
  • Okra
  • Cucumber

Operations

500 broilers per cycle (3 cycles/year), 200 layers, vegetables on 0.5 hectares, with plans for irrigation expansion.

Marketing

Direct household sales, supply to shops, restaurants and schools, supported by branding and social media.

Financial Summary

Startup Investment: D506,000
Total Revenue: D1,720,000
Operating Costs: D458,000
Annual Net Profit: D1,262,000 (73% margin)

Risk Mitigation

Vaccination, biosecurity, bulk feed purchasing, product diversification and solar-powered irrigation.

Growth Plans

Expand to over 1,000 birds per cycle, 2–3 hectares of vegetables, value addition, and formal business registration.

Conclusion

BS Farm is positioned to become a profitable and sustainable agribusiness while contributing to food security and economic development in The Gambia.
